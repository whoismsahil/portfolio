Cambly Website Re-design

  1. Home
  2. Portfolio
  3. Cambly Website Re-design

The Brief:

Re-designing Cambly Inc. current website, implementing modern visuals and minimal feel.



The Biggest Challenge:

The current website is alredy used by over a million to learn English. Redesigning something complex will affect the users.



The Purpose:

The current website consists of mismatched colors pallete and low fidelity visual elements. Usage of basic stock images and less interactive card layout.



Roles & Responsibilities:

It is a personal project, so, it is just me. It is conducted remotely. I was the one responsible for taking surveys, research, image production, wireframing, prototyping & testing.



Scope & Constraints:

During the conception, the majority of the target was the youth, who are seeking a good platform to learn English. Later on the target audience was changed from just youths to all age groups.



Process:

Conducting user interviews and user surveys. Creating rough hand drawn sketches on paper and whiteboards. Wireframing the sketches, and creating the final design/prototype.



The Outcome:

Being just the single member, from doing research to create the final design.

Learned all the aspects of working remotely & alone. Keeping the consistency to create the better product and solving the user experience related problems.



Process




Breaking down the product and refining the problem in the current design by doing user interviews:



3 out of 10 user problems came out to be the top.



Images:

Use of interactive illustrations instead of stock images.

Cards:

Use of modern layout cards and carousel.

Color:

Use of vibrant yet minimal color pallete to sooth the experience on the website.




Conducting user surveys:



Finding out the best font pairing was a challenge. Instagram poll with different font pairing was used to select the font.


Selecting the illustrations with the correct feel and modifying it to make it suitable for all the target audience & keeping it on the track of company's vision as well.




Hand drawn sketches:



Low fidelity hand drawn sketches of the product for getting the general overview to tackle the main user related problems.





Wireframes:



Use of wireframing software to create high-fidelity wireframes, an overview of the final product.





Prototype:



Interactive prototyping on the basis of the sketches and wireframes. Implementation of the different elements like, colors, fonts, texts, images, illustration and candy elements.
